ABOUT US

We all at some point or other felt that there is something missing in our current education system, although with all the current level of development. We miss our Gurukul system of teaching, where there was focus on overall development of the personality rather than only on technical aspect (only for earning) in current system. At the same time, we also cannot ignore the importance of technology. So, we need to create a special blend (mixture) which combine the best of both the systems.


Hence, we are here with the solution, PATHAKS’ NEO-GURUKUL. Pathaks in Sanskrit means the LEARNERS. Neo means revived or upgraded form of something. Hence Neo-Gurukul is the upgraded version (New Generation) Gurukul, combining the best of the tradition Gurukul System with current technological development so as to suit the need of the hour.
